Who We Are?

We have been serving you since 2006 in the UAE.

QDRY Tents & Metals Industry is one of the leading tent manufacturers, suppliers, and rental companies in the UAE. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we specialize in delivering premium tent and shade solutions tailored to a wide range of needs. Our offerings include car parking shades, outdoor sun shades, party tents, tensile structures, Arabic majlis, camping tents, exhibition setups, and more.

What types of tents and shades do you offer?
We provide a wide range including event tents, camping tents, car parking shades, pergolas, dome tents, and Arabic majlis setups. Each product is tailored to meet various residential, commercial, and industrial needs.
Can I customize the design or size of the tent or shade?
Absolutely. We offer full customization in terms of size, material, color, and structure to match your specific requirements.
What is the typical delivery and installation timeframe?
Most standard projects are completed within 3 to 7 days, depending on the complexity and customization required.
How durable are your products against UAE weather conditions?
Our tents and shades are engineered to withstand high temperatures, sandstorms, and heavy winds, ensuring year-round reliability.
